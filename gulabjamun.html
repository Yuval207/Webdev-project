Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a very popular Indian sweet made with khoya, milk solids, sugar, rose water and cardamom powder. Indeed it is a favorite Indian sweet for many of us. In parties, weddings and even in an Indian dinner meal, gulab jamun happens to be one of the sweets served after the meals.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

5 servings

Tags : Milk product Dessert Food Favourite sweet dish

instructions

step 1

MAKING THE DOUGH:ake 1 cup khoya or mawa (200 grams) in a bowl. Soft khoya also known as ‘daap ka khoya’ or ‘chikna khoya’ is used. Mash it very well. There should be no lumps or small bits or pieces in the khoya.

step 2

Then add ¾ cup or 100 grams grated paneer, fine rava (semolina), 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour, ¼ teaspoon baking powder and ½ teaspoon cardamom powder to the mashed khoya. And mix well.

step 3

Add 1 tablespoon milk and gather together to form a dough with milk. Cover the dough and set aside for 30 minutes.

step 4

MAKING SUGAR SOLUTION: Mix 1.75 cups (250 grams) sugar in 1 cup water. On a medium-low flame, heat the sugar solution till it becomes sticky

step 5

Add rose water and stir. Set the sugar solution aside. On cooling, if the sugar syrup crystallizes, then just add 2 to 3 tablespoons water and warm the syrup again. Stir while warming the syrup. The sugar crystals will disappear.

step 6

After 30 minutes, make small balls from the dough without cracks. Cover the dough balls and set aside. Heat oil until it is medium-hot. Lower the flame to a medium-low or low and wait for a minute. Then gently place the dough balls in the oil.

step 7

Remove the fried dough balls and then place them on kitchen paper towels to remove extra oil. Now put these balls in the sugar syrup you made on low flame for 1-2 minutes,so that it absorbs the syrup.

step 8

Serve gulab jamun warm or at room temperature. You can also chill them and serve them cold. Garnish them with rose petals or almond slivers.

ingredients

1 heaped cup of Khoya(milk solids)

2.5 tablespoons maida(all purpose flour)

0.25 teaspoon baking and cardamom powder

2-4 tablespoons of milk

2 tablespoons of paneer

Oil aur ghee as needed for frying

1 teaspoon of rose water

1.25-1.5 cups of sugar

3/4 cup water

tools

Deep pot

Wide Pot

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
