Veg Pulav

A pulao is a one pot dish of rice and vegetables or a protein cooked together with aromatics (onions, garlic, ginger etc), spices and herbs. There are many variations of making a pulao,such as it can be vegetarian or non vegeatrian and the ingredients may also change. For example:including Peas pulao, Kashmiri pulao, Tawa pulao, Paneer pulao,etc.

prep time

25 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

5 servings

Tags : Vegetarian Lunch Rice food

instructions

step 1

First wash and soak 1 cup basmati rice in 2 cups water. In a heavy pan heat 1 teaspoon ghee and 2 teaspoon oil. Add whole spices – 1 dry bay leaf, 2 cloves, 2 green cardamoms, 1 cinnamon stick, 1 star anise, 1 mace flower and 6-8 cashews. Fry until cashews are golden brown.

step 2

Add 2 green chillies slit cut along with 1 large onion finely sliced. Fry onions until translucent and add 1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste. Cook for 2-3 mins until there is no raw smell.

step 3

Add ½ cup mixed vegetables chopped (1 small carrot, 4-5 french beans, 1 small potato and handful of green peas – fresh/frozen).

step 4

Cook for 3-4 mins and then add ¼ cup finely chopped mint leaves. Cook for another minute.

step 5

Add water used to soak basmati rice. Let the water come to a rolling boil, add salt as needed.

step 6

Now add soaked basmati rice and mix well. Cook covered on medium flame for 4-5 mins and then reduce the heat, continue cooking covered for 10-12 mins until the rice is soft & cooked.

step 6

Let it rest for 5-10 mins before fluffing it up with a fork. Serve hot with any side dish of your choice.

ingredients

1 cup Basmati Rice

½ cup Mixed Vegetables Chopped Carrots, French Beans, Potato, Green Peas, Cauliflower,Onions

1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste

2 teaspoon Oil and 1 teaspoon Ghee

2 Green Cardamoms,2 Cloves and 1 Cinnamon Stick

2 cups Water

2-3 Green Chillies adjust per spice preference

6-8 Cashews

¼ cup Mint Leaves Finely Chopped

Salt as needed

tools

Pressure cooker
